About

Now I am forging my bioinformatician soul and looking for ways to move from study to actions. I work in prof. Severinov's lab, where I study the CRISPR-Cas systems using NGS methods. I have now found myself making the most of my experience in developing my own bioinformatics tools and teaching.

I feel part of the global bioinformatics community and I want to do my bit in this area.

Currently I have 2 goals:

  1. To develop as a bioinformatician. I am considering doing a PhD in Europe in computational biology, or working in a knowledge-intensive industry.
  2. To develop as a teacher. I've already done my short course on workflow organization (Snakemake, Git, GitHub) and I am going to be a dad leading instructor on the year-long python course in the Bioinformatics institute!
